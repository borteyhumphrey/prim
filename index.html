PIECE 01

The First Of Many

Surprises come in many forms, you know You don’t have me all figured out, as you think Here’s me showing you more of me I write As the weeks turn to months and the months turn to years I’ll write many pieces for you and for us It’ll highlight our highs and lows The joys and sad moments I’ll be here through it with you I’ll send you flowers another time Today I wanted to share a part of me I’ve kept hidden When you read this, I know you’ll be eager to share your thoughts But hold it in Bask in the truth that you have someone who genuinely adores you Relish it, I’d say In the coming weeks before I’m with you again I’ll hold onto memories of your beautiful smile Sitting with you in church Sitting on the sandy beaches of your not-so-favorite beach I’m wearing the Florida t-shirt you got me as I write this I want you to know I love you I’ll most likely start saying that at the end of every call I promised not to throw around those words loosely, so believe me when I say it Some days it’ll be wrapped in jokes and loud laughter Some days it’ll be sitting in silence - my usual self, staring at you being your loud, usual self Other days, it’ll be wrapped in a frown when you piss me off So I can safely say that I do love you And this only took about 6 minutes to write Right after you read Romans with me Paul inspired me, I guess Not you cheesing at that line This is the first of many pieces I’ll send your way

Just because emerald green is your favorite color.
Happy Valentine's Day ♥
- Love, Humphrey.
